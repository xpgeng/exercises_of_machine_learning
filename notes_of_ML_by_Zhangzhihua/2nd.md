# 随机向量

## Random Vectors and Basic Properties
- random vectors
- CDF
- absolutely continous
    - CDF 用pdf积分来表示.
    - 即, CDF的导数是pdf
    - Remark: 向量->多重积分
    - pdf >= 0
    - P = 1 与pdf的关系
- discrete 
    - random vector 取可数个点
    - pmf(probability mass function)
        - f(xi) = P(X=xi)
    - 积分变**累加**
    - support S = {x | f(x)>0}
- Marginal and Conditional distribution
